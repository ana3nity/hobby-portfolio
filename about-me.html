About Me

I am a proud career changer with 5 years of experience in health and 4 years with nonprofits. These plethora of transferrable skills are the foundation to my skills as a UX/UI Designer.


I've always been nerdy and creative and make a big career change to transfer into tech where my skills would thrive. I've been an official UX/UI Designer since 2022 and since then I have been accumulating valuable experiences from nonprofits, contractual work, and internships. I'm excited to see where my career take me!
