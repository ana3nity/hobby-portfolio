Recreation of the Site Map

In order to resolve the navigational issues on the website, the UX Design team worked together to decide on making a navigation bar from scratch by rearranging the original site map.

Unfortunately the 1st Design Team only had 2 weeks to finish the project. But FAJ stakeholders expressed they would offer a stipend/honorarium to whoever wants to help finish the entire website.

After recruiting new UX Designers, we picked up from where the 1st Design Team last left off in the Summer of 2023.

Before revisiting the wireframes, we had to have a discussion with stakeholders to confirm the Site Map

Turns out, not everyone in the organization knew which programs were still active and functioning. Further investigation with staff members led to the new, approved Site Map.

New UI Style Guide based on the Philippine National Flower:
The Sampaguita

The UI/Visual Designer of our team came up with the new color palette of the website.

These colors felt closer to FAJ’s current pivot towards themes of Mental Health and Healing while still keeping true to our culture.

Validating Revamped Designs with Usability Testing,
Once Again

We had to validate the new user flows by conducting:

  • 1 round of Usability Testing, 5 participants total
  • All ranging from different needs from interest in Youth programs, Tenant Rights Resources, to Immigration Resources) to gauge the ease of use of the Iterated site map & wireframes.

*Conducted by the 2nd Design Team

Results

Both users of FAJ’s website & stakeholder were satisfied with the new site map layout and UI Style Guide of the High-Fidelity Prototype. Wordpress Implementation and website launch was scheduled in June 2023.

Takeaways

Key Learnings

  • Being a apart of 2 design teams; transferring work to another team with a 2 month gap period; finding and recruiting UX Designers to continue project.
  • Transitioning into a Project lead role for the 2nd team.
  • 1st paid UX project; battling perfectionism & understanding the need to compromise & negotiate.
  • ULearning curves of learning low-code websites such as Wordpress and only using free plugins - stakeholders expressed limited budget.
  • Working with stakeholders that don’t understand technologies very well; delivering information in a way that doesn’t overwhelm them.

The Impact

  • Within the first 2 weeks, FAJ received $500 donation for their 50th Anniversary and 2 contacts interested in becoming members.
  • As the 50th Anniversary in October 2023 is coming up, FAJ is steadily garnering interested volunteers and 50th Anniversary donations.
