Ana-Louise Trinidad

User Experience Designer

I am a proud career changer with 5 years of experience in Healthcare and 4 years of experience in nonprofits.

I blend my passions for critical community spaces and User Experience skills to nonprofits and organizations for just causes as well as family-owned, small businesses. I desire to use technology to provide solutions for marginalised communities.

My passion for gaming and user experience drives me to contribute my skills to this exciting industry. With a focus on critical thinking, I aim to develop solutions that improve the user experience for players, creating more engaging and enjoyable gaming experiences.
